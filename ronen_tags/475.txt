Hijacked Sudanese plane leaves Cyprus for Britain .
LARNACA , Cyprus 1996-08-27
A <LOCATION>Sudan</LOCATION> Airways plane with 199 passengers and crew which was hijacked to <LOCATION>Cyprus</LOCATION> took off from <LOCATION>Larnaca</LOCATION> airport after refuelling and headed to Britain early on Tuesday , witnesses said .
Police said an unknown number of hijackers were on board the Airbus 310 which was hijacked on its way from <LOCATION>Khartoum to Amman in Jordan</LOCATION> .
One had threatened to blow it up unless it was refuelled and they were taken to London where they intended to surrender and seek political asylum .
